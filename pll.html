Practical Little Life

A lifestyle blog for new/existing moms. The client aimed to focus on product reviews and integrate her social media profiles while maintaining a genuine and aesthetically pleasing design. With a clean and minimalistic approach, I created a prototype in Figma and subsequently built the site. The homepage features a custom-coded template, while single posts utilize Gutenberg blocks for easy content management.

Product Template Build

To meet the client's request for consistent product page layouts, I developed a template that can be used for every product. This template simplifies page management by allowing the client to add new products and input the necessary content into predefined fields, with the template automatically handling the layout and styling.
