Rubberlogix

Rubberlogix is a rubber flooring distributor that creates products from recycled tires. The client wanted to maintain their existing design while upgrading certain elements. Using Elementor, I enhanced the site to better showcase their diverse product range and uphold their eco-friendly branding. The project was completed in just a few weeks and quickly went live.

Key Features & Contributions

Header/Footer Redesign & Build

Given the extensive product range, I designed a comprehensive megamenu to showcase both product lines and individual items. The goal was to create a fully responsive and modern site, and the mobile view of the megamenu reflects this objective with a unique, tailored design for RubberLogix.

Product Template Build

To meet the client's request for consistent product page layouts, I developed a template that can be used for every product. This template simplifies page management by allowing the client to add new products and input the necessary content into predefined fields, with the template automatically handling the layout and styling.
